Frequently asked

How do I export a script?

Open any project, tap the share icon in the top right, then choose PDF or Final Draft (.fdx). You can share via Files, Mail, AirDrop, or any cloud.

Does Penova work offline?

Yes. Penova is fully offline. Nothing is sent to any server. Everything you write lives on your device.

Where are my scripts stored?

Locally on your device via Apple's SwiftData. Turn on iCloud device backup in iOS Settings to keep your work safe across restores.

How do I use Quick Capture?

Tap the microphone icon on the Home screen. The first time, iOS will ask for microphone and speech recognition permission. Both are on-device when your iPhone supports it.

How do I delete a project?

Long-press a project card in the Scripts tab and tap Delete, or open the project detail, tap the ··· menu, and choose Delete. Deletions remove all episodes, scenes, and characters tied to that project.

Can I get my money back?

Refunds are handled by Apple. See reportaproblem.apple.com to request one.

Something's broken.

Please email with your iPhone model, iOS version, and what you were doing when it happened. A screenshot helps.
