Privacy Policy

Your scripts belong to you.

Effective date: 22 April 2026

The short version

Penova does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Everything you write stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. Penova does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. All scripts, projects, characters, and notes you create stay on your device.

Device permissions

Penova asks for the following permissions only for features you actively use:

  • Microphone — used only while Quick Capture is recording your dictation. No audio is stored or transmitted off your device.
  • Speech Recognition — used to transcribe your dictation into text. When supported by your device, this is performed on-device. No audio or transcription is sent to us.

Data storage

Your scripts are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud device backup enabled on your iPhone, iOS may include Penova's data in your device backup. That backup is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is not accessed by us.

Third-party services

Penova does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or any third-party services that collect user data. There is no SDK inside the app other than Apple's own frameworks.

Children's privacy

Penova is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently received information about a child, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Material changes will be called out in the next app update's release notes.
